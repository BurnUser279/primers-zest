Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Discretion

Primer's Zest operates on a foundation of absolute discretion. We understand that our members entrust us with sensitive information, including medical history and financial details. This policy outlines how we protect that trust.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our elite concierge services and maintain a secure community, we collect:

  • Personal Identity: Full name, verified email, and mobile number.
  • Vetting Documents: Government-issued Identification (Passport, Driver's License) to confirm legal identity.
  • Financial Standing: Proof of funds or relevant financial documentation for VIP tier eligibility.
  • Professional Context: Corporate affiliations and professional credentials.
  • Medical History: Required strictly for specialized healthcare concierge requests.

3. Data Sovereignty & Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We do not use third-party tracking pixels or sell your information to advertisers. Access to sensitive data is strictly limited to authorized "Global Admin" identities and is audited for security.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Members have the right to request a full export of their data or permanent deletion of their account and all associated records through the Resolution Centre.
