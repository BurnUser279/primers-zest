The Official Member Handbook & Code of Conduct

Welcome. By reading this handbook, you have successfully passed our uncompromising vetting process and have been granted access to an elite sanctuary.

This organization was founded on a singular premise: to provide a secure, uninhibited, and strictly private environment for successful professionals to explore their lifestyle dynamics and network freely. To maintain this sanctuary, we operate under a strict, non-negotiable Code of Conduct.

I. THE CORE TENET: PRIVACY & SECURITY

Your privacy is our highest priority, and protecting the privacy of your fellow members is your absolute responsibility.

  • The Device Ban: Upon arrival at any club venue, private charter, or gala, all mobile phones, smartwatches, and recording devices must be handed over at the door. There are zero exceptions to this rule. Devices are stored in secure, individually locked RFID-blocking cases until your departure.
  • The Veil of Anonymity: You will encounter high-profile individuals, executives, and recognizable figures within our walls. You must treat their presence with absolute discretion. Speculating on a member's identity, profession, or background is considered in poor taste. Anonymity underscores the party’s strongest tenet.
  • Digital Data Minimization: The club only collects the minimum data necessary for your verification and billing. We protect your digital footprint vigorously. In return, you must never screenshot member chats, forward private messages, or discuss who attended any event outside approved club channels.

II. CODE OF CONDUCT: ON-PREMISES (EVENTS & GALAS)

Our events range from luxury charity galas to uninhibited, sensory-immersive after-dark parties. Regardless of the event's nature, the following rules apply:

  • Enthusiastic Consent: This is the bedrock of our community. Any physical or intimate interaction must be preceded by clear, enthusiastic, and sober consent. Never assume that interest, a conversation, the club ring, or attendance at an event means automatic consent. Consent can be revoked at any time, for any reason, without question.
  • Graceful Rejection: If a member declines an invitation to converse, dance, or engage further, you must accept the "no" immediately and graciously. Persistence after a decline is strictly forbidden.
  • Event-Specific Etiquette: Many of our events have strict thematic or dress codes. Adhering to these codes is mandatory, as it contributes to the immersive experience for everyone.
  • Respect for Staff and Talent: Our waitstaff, security personnel, and booked models are elite professionals operating under strict NDAs. They are to be treated with the utmost respect.

III. CODE OF CONDUCT: OFF-PREMISES & PUBLIC ENGAGEMENT

The lifestyle does not end when you leave the venue, but how you navigate the public world dictates your continued membership.

  • Public Encounters: If you recognize a fellow member in the mainstream world, do not acknowledge them as a club member. You must allow them to initiate contact. If they do not, you must pretend you have never met.
  • The Insignia Protocol: Members who wish to be identified by peers in public may wear the club's signature insignia (e.g., the Primer's Zest Ring). If you spot this insignia, you may approach and politely start a conversation. However, remember that the insignia is merely an invitation to network, not a guarantee of consent for anything further.
  • Digital Discretion: You may not post locations, details, or internal club operations on any public social media platform.

IV. DISPUTES & REPORTING

If you have a concern, report it privately to the administration. Do not argue publicly, do not retaliate, and do not attempt to shame another member. Reports should include only necessary facts: what happened, when it happened, where it happened, and who was involved. The club keeps reports strictly confidential and investigates them fairly to protect everyone's dignity and privacy.

V. ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY

Membership is a privilege, not a right. You will be removed from the club, and your annual fee permanently forfeited if you engage in any of the following:

  • Breaching privacy (e.g., smuggling devices, outing members, taking unauthorized screenshots).
  • Violating the strict rules of enthusiastic consent.
  • Displaying sloppy, aggressive, or chaotic intoxication.
  • Financial default or attempting to bypass required KYC identity verification.

The intense vetting process and strict rules you have navigated to get here are the very mechanisms that keep you safe. By adhering to this handbook, you help us maintain a flawless ecosystem where imagination, luxury, and freedom can thrive without consequence.

Welcome to Primer's Zest. Enjoy the escape.
